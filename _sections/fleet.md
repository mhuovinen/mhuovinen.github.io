Three classes of vessels, each tuned for a different kind of day on the water.

- **Harbor Class**  
  28-32 ft cruisers, ideal for day trips and lesson progression. Easy handling with full electronics.
- **Bluewater Class**  
  34-40 ft sailing yachts with overnight cabins, galley kitchens, and advanced navigation packages.
- **Sunset Class**  
  Luxury powerboats for sunset cruises, harbor tours, and hosting small gatherings on the water.
